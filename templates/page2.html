Pattern-based Authentication

Goal: Evaluate the usability and intuitiveness of a pattern-based authentication system.

Procedure:

  • Participants are shown a 3x3 grid (similar to Android pattern lock).
  • They create a pattern and confirm it.
  • After a delay task, they must redraw the same pattern to gain access.
  • Time, error rate, and number of attempts will be recorded.
