String Password Authentication

Goal: Evaluate the usability and memorability of typed passwords.

Procedure:

  • Create your own typed password following these rules:
    • At least 8 characters long
    • At least one uppercase letter (A–Z)
    • At least one lowercase letter (a–z)
    • At least one number (0–9)
  • Confirm the same password immediately after creating it.
  • After a short unrelated task, use your password to log in.
  • Time, error rate, and number of attempts will be recorded.
