Image Sequence Authentication

Goal: Evaluate the usability and intuitiveness of image-based authentication.

Procedure:

  • Participants are shown a grid of images.
  • They select a sequence of at least 4 images to use as their password.
  • After a delay task, they are asked to reselect the images in the same set.
  • Time, error rate, and number of attempts will be recorded.
